<p><p>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market is primarily divided into two types: hybrid navigation and electromagnetic systems. Hybrid navigation systems combine various technologies, such as optical and electromagnetic tracking, to enhance accuracy and reliability in surgical procedures. Electromagnetic navigation systems, on the other hand, utilize magnetic fields to track surgical instruments in real-time, offering precise positioning without line-of-sight limitations. Both types aim to improve surgical outcomes, reduce complications, and streamline procedures in various medical fields.</p></p>
