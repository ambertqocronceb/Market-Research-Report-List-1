<p><p>Package on Package (PoP) technology integrates multiple ICs into a single package, enhancing performance and minimizing space. The traditional PoP market involves stacking a memory chip atop a logic chip for compact design. PSfcCSP offers a smaller footprint with Flip Chip integration, utilizing solder bumps for connection. Through-Mold-Via (TMV) technology allows for vertical interconnections within the mold compound, optimizing space further. Exposed-die TMV features visible dies for enhanced thermal performance and improved electrical characteristics, catering to high-performance applications.</p></p>
