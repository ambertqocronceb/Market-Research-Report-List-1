<p><p>The edible animal fats market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and semi-solid fats. Liquid fats, such as fish oil and certain cooking oils, are primarily used for frying and dressing. Solid fats, like lard and tallow, are often utilized in baking and food processing for their texture and flavor contributions. Semi-solid fats, such as shortening or margarine, provide versatility in culinary applications, enhancing both the mouthfeel and stability of various food products.</p></p>
