<p><strong>The Interferometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Fizeau Interferometers</li><li>Mach-Zehnder Interferometer</li><li>Michelson Interferometer</li><li>Fabry-Perot Interferometer</li><li>Sagnac Interferometer</li><li>Twyman-Green Laser Interferometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interferometry market encompasses various types of devices used for precise measurement and analysis. Laser Fizeau Interferometers excel in surface flatness and optical testing. Mach-Zehnder Interferometers are optimal for phase measurement and signal processing. Michelson Interferometers are widely used in spectroscopy and optical coherence. Fabry-Perot Interferometers provide high-resolution wavelength measurements through multiple beam interference. Sagnac Interferometers are key in rotation sensing applications, while Twyman-Green Laser Interferometers are utilized for surface testing and quality control, thanks to their sensitivity and versatility.</p></p>
