<p><ul><li>Double Rollers</li><li>Four Rollers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal roller mills are categorized primarily into two types: double rollers and four rollers. Double roller mills feature two grinding rolls, making them suitable for small to medium-scale operations where space and efficiency are critical. In contrast, four roller mills utilize an additional pair of rolls to enhance grinding capabilities, providing increased throughput and finer product consistency. Both types are employed in various industries, including agriculture and materials processing, to optimize the milling process and improve overall production efficiency.</p></p>
