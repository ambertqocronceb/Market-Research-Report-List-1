<p><strong>The Mainline Rail Signalling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Train Control</li><li>Communication Based Train Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mainline rail signalling systems market is primarily categorized into two types: Traditional Train Control (TTC) and Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC). TTC relies on fixed signals and trackside equipment to manage train movements, ensuring safety and efficiency based on pre-defined schedules. In contrast, CBTC utilizes advanced communication technologies for real-time data exchange between trains and control centers, enabling dynamic train operations, reduced headways, and enhanced safety measures. Both systems aim to optimize rail network performance and improve passenger services.</p></p>
